My research centers on <b>high-fidelity and differentiable multi-physics simulation</b> as a computational foundation for physically interactive systems, spanning graphics and robotics.

As Richard Feynman once said, <i>“What I cannot create, I do not understand.”</i> Guided by this idea, my long-term goal is to build next-generation physics engines — systems that deepen our understanding of the physical world and serve as digital twins that enable reliable prediction and informed decision-making.
